Model Description:
PROCESS.Example_D_03.p.FMI_Exp
The model represents the process of evaporation, cooling and storage of a fluid mixture of water and ethanol. 
The model includes a flash tank, a cooler, a gas tank and the required control loops for the correct process operation.
The most interesting output variables are those regulated by the four PID controllers: 
Level and pressure at the flash tank, temperature at the cooler and pressure at the gas tank.
