Model Description:
PIPELIQTRAN_EXAMPLES.Example_Water_Circulation_System.default.Case4product is adequate. 
This model evaluates the behavior of a Circulating Water System of a Power Plant. 
The case consists of the sequential start-up of both pumps with the system full of water except the vacuum breakers.
The purpose of this analysis is to check the correct opening time of the pump relief valves so that system pressures do not exceed the maximum allowable pressure.
